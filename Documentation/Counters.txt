Death Toll 
    One Death Toll counter is removed at the end of the unit's controller's turn. When the last Death Toll counter is removed from
        a unit, that unit is destroyed.

Cooldown
    One Cooldown counter is removed at the end of the unit's controller's turn. Units with at least one Cooldown counter may not
        use activated abilites. Triggered and Static abilities function normally.
